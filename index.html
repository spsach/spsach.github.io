Satya Sachdeva is a hands-on CTO and AI Strategist with over 25 years of experience delivering innovative solutions to complex business problems across Financial Services, Healthcare, and High-Tech industries.

As Co-Founder of Previsant, Satya has pioneered the Adaptive Intelligence platform — unifying Gen AI, Graph Databases, AI Agents, MCP (Model Context Protocol), and Relational Databases into next-generation tools for healthcare fraud, waste, and abuse prevention.

He has provided thought leadership guiding organisations from traditional BI to cutting-edge AI, including leveraging quantum computing for pharmaceutical and medical-device next-gen capabilities.
